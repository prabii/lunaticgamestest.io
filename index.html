About Us

Lunatic Games is the first Blockchaingaming edition, promising to upgrade its players' lifestyles in exchange for their dedication and engagement.

We are building a browser-based web3 game where users will need to stake $SLNT tokens to play.

Designed with unreal Engine 5 and in-house resources The game framework project provides the players with a greek history with countless spells, Societies abilities and environment.

NFTs in-game are obtained through rare drops and in-game events. Our sole mission is to ‘Add Value’ to our players lives!

How To Play

  • 01

    Lunatic Games

    It is action pack RPG game where You will start as witch Trying to get her belonging back. You can choose between different characters further in the game. You have to collect all the relics and help the witch to take her revenge..

  • 02

    Leveling up

    By exploring and killing enemies gain more xp and upgrade you skills to become more power full to progress ahead in the game. Find relics to increase your atributes significantly.

  • 03

    Gathering Collectibles

    Collect as many collectibles as possible. The healing potion, MANA, and cats are the ultimate energy sources for the witch. The collectibles will assist gamers with improving their performance.

  • 04

    Using Your Magical Powers For Defensive & Offensive Attack

    Player have to use characters ability to progress in the game. Player have mainly 3 abilities and 1 ultimate ability that differs from character to character. Player have to choses suitable character for their play style to defeat enemies
