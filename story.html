BackStory

THE SOCIETY ASTUTELY GLORIFIES PREJUDICES UNDER THE AEGIS OF HONOUR.

THE MOON WITCH, WHO WAS DEPRIVED OF ENTERING THE HUMAN WORLD LIVED PEACEFULLY WITH HER MOST PRECIOUS POSSESSION- HER RARE CATS. HER FATE, HOWEVER, UNFOLDS DIFFERENTLY.

ON THE FULL MOON NIGHT, WHILE PERFORMING A RITUALISTIC SPELL, SOMETHING WENT WRONG AND HER CATS DISAPPEARED. SHE MUSTERED HER COURAGE AND VENTURED INTO THE HUMAN WORLD.

UNAWARE OF THAT FICKLENESS AND MISTAKEN PERCEPTION, SHE FINDS THAT THE VILLAGERS ARE READY TO ATTACK HER. SHE SURRENDERS TO SAVE HER CATS FROM CAPTIVATION. THE VILLAGERS TIE HER WITH THORN WIRE AND STAB HER TILL HER LAST BREATH.

THE VILLAGERS LEAVE HER TO DIE, BUT NO ONE CAN CONTRADICT GOD'S WILL. HER CATS-HER ONLY STRENGTH LICKED HER FACE WITH LOVE WHICH AWAKENS HER DYING SOUL. THE MOONLIGHT FALLS ON HER AND FREE HER FROM THE CLUTCHES.

SHE CASTS THE DEADLIEST AND STRONGEST OF SPELLS ASKS FOR THE POWERS FROM HER ANCESTORS AND AWAKENS THE POWERFUL AND DEADLY –THE LUNIAL ARMY!
